About this app

It was designed by the partners to help partners cover their shift

Use your best judgment and contact the person who wants to take your shift

This app is ment as a quick tool to get in touch with partners that would like to switch shifts with you.

We suggest always contacting the other person via phone instead of just trusting that the person will show up

We are not responsible or held accountable for missed shifts

Partners working for license stores cant trade shifts due to their own store restrictions
